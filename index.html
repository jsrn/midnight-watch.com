Midnight Watch

A Free Ultima Online Roleplaying Server

Midnight Watch is a low(ish) fantasy roleplaying server. We aim to bring you exciting and atmospheric gameplay in a setting full of danger and scarcity with a strong player driven economy, meaningful politics, and a world in which your characters can stake their claim through grit, determination, cunning and/or violence.

Key Features

  • Centralised gameplay in a brand new city. Always easy to find someone to talk to.
  • Time based skill system to take the grinding out of gameplay and reward specialisation.
  • Player driven economy: Fewer NPCs and enhanced crafting means you can really make your mark.
  • Staff events using all of the powers that a GM can provide.

How to Play

  1. Read the rules and roleplaying guidelines.
  2. Read the character creation guide and come up with an appropriate backstory for your character.
  3. Download the client bundle.
  4. Download Razor.
  5. Connect to:
    • Host: play.midnight-watch.com
    • Port: 2593
  6. Log in and have fun!
Joining the server constitutes agreement to abide by the setting and rules in place. Failure to adhere to the rules and roleplay guidelines may result in loss of access to the sever.
