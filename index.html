Midnight Watch

An Ultima Online roleplaying shard

The second iteration of Project Brains, the server brought to you by refugees from the Ultima Online roleplaying community. We run a pre-AoS base, modified to fit the rules of engagement that many of you know (and hopefully love).

Why?

Most of us hail, in one way or another, from the excellent (and still going! Check it out!) Europa server. However, as time went on and people went on their own journeys, the draw of struggling against the game to create the environment we wanted diminished. We wanted to create a new roleplaying experience, with more focus on roleplaying and less on working around the game.

What?

  • Centralised gameplay in a new city to get more mileage out of fewer online players, without being held back by our considerable attachment to Europa lore.
  • Time based skill system to take the grinding out of gameplay, and reward specialisation.
  • Player driven economy, where you can truly carve out your piece.
  • Staff events using all of the powers that a GM can provide.
  • A brand new city and a new twist on an old map.
